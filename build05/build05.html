Visit Ibadan

Experience culture, history, and vibrant life

Top three activities to do in Ibadan

Explore Cocoa House

Cocoa House is Nigeria’s first skyscraper
and a symbol of the city’s historic cocoa wealth.
A great place to admire Ibadan’s legacy.

Relax at Agodi Gardens

Agodi Gardens is perfect for picnics,
sightseeing, and spending time with friends
or family in a lush, green environment.

Climb Bower’s Tower

Bower’s Tower gives you a breathtaking panoramic view
of the entire Ibadan city; a must-see for visitors.
